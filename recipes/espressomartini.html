Espresso Martini

Description

An espresso martini is made with both coffee liqueur and fresh espresso, so you’ll get the pick-me-up you desire no matter the time of day.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 3/4 ounce coffee liqueur
  • 1/2 ounce crème de cacao
  • 1 ounce espresso, chilled
  • 3 coffee beans, for garnish
